How to play

Collect points by hovering over circles, in order, and hitting the 'x' key. The closer the ring is to the circle, the more points you get.

Over time, your health depletes. Recover health by collecting points. If you hit the 'x' key while the cursor is out of the circle, you lose health.

The game is over when your health bar reaches zero or the song finishes
